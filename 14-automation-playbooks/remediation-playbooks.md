Automated Remediation Playbooks

Remediation: Public Storage Exposure
- Detect public access.
- Restrict access.
- Notify owner.
- Disable public ACLs.

Remediation: High-Risk IAM Role
- Detect admin roles assigned to regular users.
- Remove excessive privileges.
- Add user to least-privilege group.

Remediation: Open Firewall Ports
- Detect port 22 or 3389 open to internet.
- Restrict to known IP or block entirely.

Remediation: Missing Encryption
- Detect unencrypted storage.
- Apply encryption key automatically.

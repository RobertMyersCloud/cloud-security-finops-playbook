Eradication and Recovery

Eradication:
- Remove malicious files or scripts.
- Terminate unauthorized access.
- Patch vulnerabilities.
- Reconfigure security groups or NSGs.
- Reset exposed credentials and secrets.

Recovery:
- Deploy clean resources from templates.
- Restore data from backups.
- Reapply monitoring and IAM controls.
- Validate environment with log reviews.
- Gradually return services to normal operation.

Validation:
- Confirm no malware or persistence remains.
- Confirm IAM roles follow least privilege.
- Confirm network segmentation is correct.

Threat Intelligence Basics

Types of Threat Intel:
- Strategic: High-level risks and trends
- Operational: Specific campaigns and TTPs
- Tactical: Indicators of compromise
- Technical: Log signatures and hashes

Common Threat Intel Sources:
- MITRE ATT&CK
- MSRC
- AWS Security Bulletins
- CISA alerts
- NIST NVD

Cloud Application:
- Apply indicators to log searches
- Create custom alert rules
- Detect cloud-specific attack techniques
